About Me

An avid thrifter, I am obsessed with all things vintage. I find inspiration in mid-century children's book illustrations and vintage color pallettes.

I run an instagram account called @sweet_walls_of_everett where I share my collection of urban photography, capturing old and interesting walls around downtown Everett.

I also make illustrations in my free time, and will eventually start selling prints in my etsy shop.
